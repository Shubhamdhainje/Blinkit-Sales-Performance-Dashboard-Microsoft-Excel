## Blinkit Sales Performance Dashboard — Microsoft Excel

A comprehensive sales analytics dashboard built entirely in Microsoft Excel — 
covering Blinkit's sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory 
distribution across outlets.
__________________________________________________________________________________________
## Project Overview

Blinkit (India's Last Minute App) operates across multiple outlet types, sizes, and 
geographic tiers. This project conducts a comprehensive business analysis of Blinkit's 
sales data to surface key insights and optimisation opportunities — delivered through 
a fully interactive Excel dashboard with slicers, pivot tables, and dynamic charts.
Business Goal: Identify which outlet types, locations, item categories, and fat content
profiles drive the most revenue — and where performance gaps exist.

## Charts & Analysis
1. Total Sales by Fat Content — Donut Chart

•	Analyses the impact of fat content (Low Fat vs Regular) on total revenue

•	Regular items dominate at $776.32K (65%) vs Low Fat at $425.36K (35%)

•	Additional KPIs (Avg Sales, No. of Items, Avg Rating) broken down by fat content

2. Total Sales by Item Type — Clutser Bar Chart

•	Identifies the best and worst performing product categories

•	Top performers: Fruits & Vegetables ($178.1K), Snack Foods ($175.4K), Household ($136K)

•	Lowest performers: Seafood ($9.1K), Breakfast ($15.6K), Starchy Foods ($21.9K)

3. Fat Content by Outlet for Total Sales — Cluster Bar Chart

•	Compares Low Fat vs Regular sales across Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 outlets

•	Tier 3 leads with $306.81K (Regular) and $165.33K (Low Fat)

4. Total Sales by Outlet Establishment — Area Chart

•	Evaluates how outlet age influences revenue from 1998 to 2022

•	Peak sales at establishment year 1998 ($204.5K); stabilised post-2015 at ~$130K

5. Sales by Outlet Size — Donut Chart

•	Analyses correlation between outlet size and total revenue

•	Medium outlets lead at $507.9K (42%), High at $444.8K (37%), Small at $249.0K (21%)

6. Sales by Outlet Location — Funnel Chart

•	Geographic revenue distribution across Tier 1, 2, and 3 locations

•	Tier 3 highest at $472.1K, Tier 2 at $393.2K, Tier 1 at $336.4K

7. All Metrics by Outlet Type — Cluster Bar Charts

•	Comprehensive breakdown of all 4 KPIs by outlet type

•	Supermarket Type1 dominates: $787.5K total sales, 5,577 items, Avg Sales $141

•	Grocery Store shows lowest volume but consistent Avg Rating of $140

## Key Business Insights

•	 Supermarket Type1 is the highest-revenue outlet type at $787.5K — nearly 5× higher 
than any other outlet type

•	 Tier 3 locations outperform Tier 1 despite being smaller markets — signalling untapped 
growth potential in smaller cities

•	 Regular fat content products outsell Low Fat 2:1 — consumer preference leans towards 
regular products despite health trends

•	 Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods are the top-selling categories — inventory 
prioritisation should reflect this

•	 Older established outlets (pre-2000) achieved peak historical sales; newer outlets 
plateau around $130K suggesting market saturation
